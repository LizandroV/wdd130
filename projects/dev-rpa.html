RPA

Through the development of RPA projects, I have managed to automate repetitive tasks and free up time and resources for higher-value activities within organizations. These projects have covered a wide range of functional areas, from data processing to inventory management and customer service. By implementing RPA solutions, I have achieved improved accuracy, reduced errors, and accelerated execution times.

Each project highlights how intelligent automation can have a significant impact on operational efficiency and cost reduction.

RPA UPDATE

Created to update information in a company system, this was a job that was done by one person and it took about 1 hour, and he did it 5 times a day, with RPA automation the work is done in 30 min and is done 20 times a day.

RPA TRANFER

Created to carry out material transfers in SAP, this task was carried out by 3 people every day, taking 2 hours, with RPA automation, this process is carried out in 40 min and helps to keep the equipment inventory updated.

RPA WEB

Created to upload information to a web portal, this task took 1 hour to be processed by a staff, with the automation by RPA the task is done in 30 min helping to reduce work times.
